About Me (My Programming Journey)

I am Fin Tech veteran with a passion for project management, client engagement and coding! The past 3 years, I have been writing back end code to increase efficiencies and create client reporting. The experience led me to complete a coding boot camp at the University of Pennsylvania.

But before I get into that, let me explain how my journey began. I started my career at La Salle University working with Microsoft Office products like Excel, Word and Access.


I quickly learned the power of the intergrated suite of applications and built a database that was used by the leadership team for Graduate Admissions. The application helped manage student enrollment, student deliverables, produce reporting and communicate customized e-mails to all students with the push of a button. It vastly improved and centralized the admissions workflow.

My skills in formula writing and report building in Excel/Access evolved to VBA and PL/SQL to solve more complex business issues, increase accuracy and drive efficiencies.

The last 12 months, my learning has mainly focused on HTML5, CSS3, Javascript (ES6), jQuery, AJAX/ APIs, Bootstrap, Materialize, Foundation, Firebase, Node.JS, MySQL, PL/SQL, MongoDB, Express, Handlebars, React, GitHub and JIRA. I plan on completing additional certificates/ training in Python, C# and Java later this year.

This next chapter of my career is exciting as I plan to integrate my passions for project management, investment services and coding to build solutions that solve business problems. The combination of my experience, education - which includes an MBA from La Salle University and Coding Certificate from the University of Pennsylvania - has given me the tools and knowledge I need to add value in any setting.
