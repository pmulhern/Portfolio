About Me

I have 15+ years of professional experience supporting Equity, Fixed Income, Derivatives, FXs, Bank Loans, Stable Value and Private Equity operations. After a decade and a half working in the investment operations space, I am transitioning to my dream career as a full-stack programmer!

I started my career at La Salle University where intellectual curiousity led me to test and build a proprietary database in

Microsoft Access. The database was exclusively used by the Assistant Director for Graduate Admissions to manage student enrollment. This database utility tracked student deliverables and had reporting and communication functionality. It vastly improved and centralized the admission workflow.


Over the years my skills in formula writing and report building in Excel/Access evolved to writing VBA scripts and SQL statements to solve business problems and drive efficient workflows.


This next chapter of my career is especially exciting given my passion for coding and building systems that help people improve the quality and timeliness of their work deliverables! The combination of experience, education and University of Pennsylvania Coding Bootcamp certificate has given me the tools and knowledge I need to be successful.
