About Me (My Programming Journey)

I have 15+ years of professional experience in Project and Operations Management supporting Equity, Fixed Income, Derivatives, FXs, Bank Loans, Stable Value and Private Equity products. After a decade and a half working in the investment operations space, I am transitioning to my dream career as a programmer!

I started my career at La Salle University where intellectual curiousity led me to build a proprietary database in Microsoft Access.


The database was exclusively used by the Assistant Director for Graduate Admissions to manage student enrollment, track student deliverables, produce managerial reporting and communicate with students. It vastly improved and centralized the admissions workflow.

Over the years my skills in formula writing and report building in Excel/Access evolved to writing VBA and SQL code to solve business problems and drive efficiencies.

This next chapter of my career is especially exciting given my passion for coding and building systems that help people improve the quality and timeliness of their work deliverables! The combination of experience, education (which includes an MBA and now a University of Pennsylvania Coding certificate) has given me the tools and knowledge I need to add value in any setting.

These past few months have focused on HTML5, CSS3, Javascript (ES6), jQuery, AJAX/ APIs, Node.JS and SQL. I plan on completing additional certificates in Python, C# and Java after the Bootcamp. This far, I have excelled in all aspects of programming and invite you to review my recent projects located on the Portfolio link above.
